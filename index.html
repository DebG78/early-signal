Early Success Detection Engine

Predict who succeeds.
Act before they don't.

Early Success treats employee success as a solvable problem — not destiny. By connecting data across three critical phases, the system spots patterns early and orchestrates interventions before people slip.

1
Hire Are we hiring the right people, through the right channels, with the right interviewers?
2
Land Are the first 90 days setting people up — or letting them drift?
3
Thrive In months 6–12, are managers investing, growth happening, and teams balanced?

Agents detect patterns and draft recommendations. Humans decide and approve. Every intervention feeds outcome data back into the engine — a closed loop that gets sharper with each cycle.
